RETAINED PROCESS

Starting with a comprehensive assessment with the management team to define the nature of the position and how it fits into your organization. We'll assemble a candidate profile taking many factors into account including experience, personality, and commitment to building a successful team. Every company has its own set of nuances and this step is essential to finding a person that will be a long-term A-player in the team.

CONTINGENCY SEARCH

Our contingency search process is a scaled version of our retained search process. We obtain a detailed view of the hiring needs and company culture. We assemble a potential list of candidates that are available and have an ongoing interest in furthering their career. Our process includes recruiting, interviewing, checking references, submission to company for interview, follow-up and the negotiation of compensation package.

RECRUITING PACKAGE

XCREEK provides a unique outsourced service to our clients in a customizable and configurable recruiting package. We provide a streamlined recruiting infrastructure that allows you to build your team and get product to market as quickly as possible while reducing your overhead and eliminating your headaches.
